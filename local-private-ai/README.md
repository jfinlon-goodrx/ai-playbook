## Decision Guide

Choose local or private AI when:

- data sensitivity makes public consumer tools a poor fit,
- the team needs an internal knowledge assistant,
- predictable cost or offline capability matters,
- or you are building enterprise guardrails around AI access.

Choose hosted models first when:

- you need the best frontier capability quickly,
- governance is already satisfied through enterprise wrappers,
- or the team is still validating the workflow before investing in infrastructure.
