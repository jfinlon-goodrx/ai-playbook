# Tech Lead Starter

## Goal

Help a tech lead or architect make AI-assisted delivery faster without losing architectural integrity, review quality, or operational trust.

## First Three Wins

1. Standardize project context and rules in one repo.
2. Pilot one vertical-slice workflow with clear review expectations.
3. Shift review effort from mechanical issues toward architecture, risk, and rollout quality.

## Working Rules

- make story intent clearer before asking for implementation
- keep risk tiers simple and visible
- automate low-judgment checks aggressively
- preserve human attention for domain, architecture, and release risk
