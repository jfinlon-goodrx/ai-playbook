# Manager Starter

## Goal

Help an engineering manager introduce AI in a way that improves cycle time and quality together, rather than creating ungoverned speed pressure.

## First Three Wins

1. Pilot one team workflow instead of announcing a broad AI rollout.
2. Track review latency, escaped defects, and release confidence alongside speed.
3. Capture successful prompts, rules, and skills in the repo.

## Working Rules

- do not optimize for prompt count or superficial velocity
- reward reusable patterns and safer workflows
- use AI to reduce friction in the system, not to skip judgment
- treat QA and platform as core enablers, not downstream gates
