# QA Starter

## Goal

Help QA and test automation engineers use AI to increase coverage, reduce manual toil, and improve risk discovery.

## First Read

- `../0-to-60-ai/19-AI-for-QA-and-Testing.md`
- `../prompts/by-task/qa/README.md`

- `../prompts/by-task/qa/test-generation.md`

## First Three Wins

1. Turn one story into a risk-based test plan.
2. Convert one repetitive manual test area into automation candidates.
3. Use AI to find missing scenarios in an existing regression suite.

## Working Rules

- test behavior, not implementation trivia
- use AI to expand edge-case coverage, not to skip validation
- keep fake data obviously fake
- stay close to business risk, auth, and integration boundaries
